About Me

I am a Senior at Andover High School who is interested in a career in a STEM-related field. In the various computer science classes I have taken I have learned object-oriented programming in Java as well as website creation and design along with some Android app development. My experience with robotics includes being a part of the FTC (first tech challenge) robotics club at my high school where I am a team leader, as well as starting my own robotics club (Seaperch). Through this journey, from building and coding robots for the FTC club to learning the engineering design process in Seaperch, I realized how robotics and software can change the world. In the FTC, my team has gone to the states competition, the past 4 years and this year we were able to progress to eleventh place. Seaperch is a robotics club I started with some of my peers at Andover High. This club takes part in the Seaperch competition hosted by the US Navy. https://seaperch-andover.netlify.com/
